Spongebob's Timeline

  • July 14th, 1986: Spongebob's birthday according to his drivers license.
  • 1989: Hillenberg realeses a comic book "The Intertidal Zone" in which many of the ideas of SpongeBob squarePants orginate from.
  • 1996: Hillenberg starts developing Spongebob Squarepants into a tv series after the cancellation of "Rocko's Modern Life". This is when Hillenberg starts to work with Tom Kenny who is the voice of SpongeBob. However, SpongeBob was originally going to be named SpongeBoy, with the series being called "SpongeBoy Ahoy!". Luckily SpongeBoy was already trademarked. Thus, the legend SpongeBob SquarePants was created.
  • May 1st, 1999: The first preview of Spongebob Squarepants ever was aired on Nickelodeon after the 1999's Kids' Choice Awards.
  • July 17th, 1999: The first full official episode of the SpongeBob SquarePant's series was premiered.
  • November 19th, 2004: The feature film "The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie" was released in theaters.
  • January 18th, 2009: Nickelodean started celebrating SpongeBob SquarePant's 10th anniversary with their first ever live reading, that they held at that year's Sundance Film Festival.
  • July 14th, 2009: Three nights before the official 10th anniversary of SpongeBob SquarePants, the one hour feature film "Square Roots: The Story of SpongeBob SquarePants" was premiered on VH1.
  • 2011: a newly discovered species of fungus was found and named after the series. Named "Spongiforma squarepantsii" after SpongeBob SquarePants.
  • February 6th, 2015: The 2nd feature film "The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge Out of Water" was released in theaters.
  • May 22nd, 2020: The 3rd feature film "The Spongebob Movie: Sponge on the Run" is expected to release in theaters.
